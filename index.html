Privacy Policy

BND Manager — Warehouse & Inventory Manager

Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Introduction

BND Manager ("the App") is a warehouse and inventory management tool for businesses. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

By using BND Manager you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

BND Manager collects only the information you enter directly into the app:

  • Name and email address — used for login and user identification
  • Business data — warehouse names, product names, order details, supplier and customer names, and financial figures

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, health data, or any financial account information.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in a Google Sheets spreadsheet and Google Firestore database that you own and control under your own Google account. BND Manager connects directly to your Google infrastructure.

We do not operate a central server that stores copies of your business data. You are in full control of where your data lives.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide the core features of the app:

  • Authenticating your login
  • Displaying inventory, orders, and financial summaries
  • Sending in-app notifications about open orders

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sheets / Google Apps Script — database storage and business logic
  • Google Firebase / Firestore — real-time data sync
  • Expo (React Native) — app framework and push notification delivery

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

Data is only transmitted to the Google services you have connected to the app, as described above.

7. Data Retention

Your data remains in your Google Sheet for as long as you choose to keep it. Deleting your Google Sheet removes all associated data. There is no separate copy held by BND Manager.

8. Children's Privacy

BND Manager is a business tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

Access to the app is protected by email and password authentication. All data transmission to Google services uses encrypted HTTPS connections. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
